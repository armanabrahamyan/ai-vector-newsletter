Listed prices mislead in nearly a third of reasoning-model comparisons

A systematic study of eight frontier reasoning models across 12 tasks finds that in 32% of model-pair comparisons, the cheaper-listed model costs more to run; reversals reach 28x. Before committing to a reasoning-model contract, run your actual workload through both candidates and measure real spend.
